# General Support & Escalation Policy

## Overview
MESA (Mines Enterprise Support & Advising Agent) provides AI-assisted first-line support. When an issue cannot be resolved through self-service or AI guidance, it is escalated to the IT Service Desk.

## IT Service Desk Contact Information

- **Phone**: 303-278-HELP (4357) — Monday–Friday, 7:30 AM–5:30 PM MST
- **Walk-in**: Coolbaugh Hall, Room 209 — Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM MST
- **Help Center**: helpcenter.mines.edu — submit tickets and browse the knowledge base 24/7
- **Emergency after-hours**: 303-278-4357 (select Emergency option)

## What Requires Immediate Escalation

Contact IT Service Desk immediately (do not wait) for:
- Suspected account compromise or unauthorized access
- Data breach or accidental exposure of sensitive data
- Ransomware or malware infection on any device
- Complete system or network outage affecting multiple users
- Loss of access to systems needed for time-sensitive academic or research work

## Standard Escalation Criteria

Your ticket will be escalated to an IT staff member if:
- The AI-provided resolution does not resolve your issue after attempting the steps
- The issue requires admin-level access or system-side configuration
- The issue affects multiple users (infrastructure problem)
- Sensitive data is involved that requires a specialist
- The issue has persisted more than 2 business days without resolution

## Response Time Expectations

| Priority | Description | Target Response |
|---|---|---|
| Critical | System outage, security incident | Within 1 hour |
| High | Single-user complete loss of access to critical system | Within 4 hours |
| Medium | Partial access issue, workaround available | Within 1 business day |
| Low | General inquiry, non-blocking issue | Within 3 business days |

## What to Include in Your Escalation

To speed up resolution when talking to IT staff, have ready:
1. Your Mines username (not your full email)
2. The system you are trying to access (Banner, Canvas, Workday, etc.)
3. The exact error message (screenshot if possible)
4. When the problem started
5. What you have already tried
6. Your device type and operating system

## Privacy Reminder
When submitting support tickets or communicating with IT, do not include:
- Your full Social Security Number
- Your complete MultiPass password
- Financial account numbers
- Any other sensitive personal information

If IT needs to verify your identity, they will do so through official channels (Mines ID number, not SSN).
