# Workday — Payroll & HR Self-Service

## Overview
Workday is Mines' HR and payroll system for faculty, staff, and student employees. Access at workday.mines.edu using MultiPass credentials.

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Cannot see pay stubs or payroll information
1. Verify you are signed in with your mines.edu account (not a personal email).
2. In Workday, navigate to: Pay → Pay Slips. If this menu is missing, your worker profile may not have payroll access.
3. New employees: payroll visibility is granted after your first pay period is processed (typically 2–4 weeks after hire date).
4. If you are a student employee, ensure your student employment contract is active in Workday.

### Direct deposit not set up or incorrect
- Navigate to: Pay → Payment Elections to add or update your bank account.
- Changes take effect on the next payroll cycle (changes submitted after the payroll cutoff apply to the following period).
- Payroll cutoff dates are posted on the HR calendar at hr.mines.edu.

### Paycheck amount appears incorrect
- Review your pay slip details: gross pay, deductions, tax withholdings.
- Check if a benefit deduction changed — open enrollment changes take effect at the start of the new benefit year.
- If a deduction seems wrong, contact HR directly: hr@mines.edu or 303-273-3250.
- Do not attempt to resolve payroll discrepancies through IT — these require HR and Payroll team involvement.

### Cannot submit timesheet
- Timesheet submission windows open/close on a schedule — check the HR calendar.
- If the "Submit" button is missing, your manager may not have approved your time entry setup.
- Bi-weekly employees must submit by Sunday 11:59 PM of the pay period end.

### Missing benefit elections in Workday
- Benefits are visible under: Benefits → Benefits → Benefit Elections.
- New hire benefit enrollment window is 30 days from hire date. After that, changes require a qualifying life event.
- Contact HR Benefits Office for questions on specific benefit plans.

### Workday login issues
- Workday uses MultiPass SSO — see the Banner Authentication article for password reset steps.
- If you see "You are not authorized," your Workday role has not been provisioned. Contact HR.

## When to Escalate
- Payroll amounts are incorrect — escalate to HR/Payroll, not IT.
- Workday access not provisioned more than 3 business days after hire.
- Timesheet submission failures that persist after browser refresh.
