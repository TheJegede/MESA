# Canvas — LMS Issues & Grade Sync Failures

## Overview
Canvas is Mines' Learning Management System (LMS). Used for course materials, assignments, grades, and communication. Access at canvas.mines.edu using MultiPass credentials.

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Grades not syncing from Canvas to Banner
- Canvas grade sync to Banner runs nightly at 2:00 AM MST.
- If grades are missing after 24 hours, the instructor must manually trigger the sync.
- Students: contact your instructor first — they initiate the sync, not IT.
- Instructors: go to Grades → Sync → Export to Banner. If the sync button is grayed out, contact IT.

### Cannot access a course in Canvas
1. Confirm the instructor has published the course (unpublished courses are invisible to students).
2. Verify your enrollment in Banner — Canvas enrollment mirrors Banner registration.
3. If enrolled in Banner but missing in Canvas, wait 2–4 hours for the sync to run.
4. If still missing after 4 hours, submit a ticket with your course name and CRN.

### Assignment submission errors
- Check file size: Canvas limit is 500MB per file.
- Supported formats depend on assignment settings — check the assignment instructions.
- If submit button is missing, the deadline may have passed or the assignment requires a different submission type (media, text, etc.).

### Canvas mobile app not loading
- Force close and reopen the app.
- Log out and log back in using your mines.edu credentials.
- Ensure your app is updated to the latest version.

### Video content not playing in Canvas
- Videos hosted in Kaltura or YouTube require a stable internet connection.
- Disable browser ad-blockers or VPN that may block media domains.
- Try downloading the video if a download link is available.

### Missing quiz or exam in Canvas
- The instructor may have set an availability window — check the course calendar.
- Contact your instructor to confirm the quiz is published and available to your section.

## When to Escalate
- Grade sync failures persist more than 48 hours after instructor action.
- Course enrollment discrepancy between Banner and Canvas after 4 hours.
- Canvas system-wide outage — check status.instructure.com.
