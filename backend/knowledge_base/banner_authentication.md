# Banner — Authentication & Login Issues

## Overview
Banner is Colorado School of Mines' student information system, used for course registration, academic records, financial aid, and student accounts. Access is via Single Sign-On (SSO) using your MultiPass credentials.

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Cannot log in to Banner
1. Verify you are using your full Mines email (username@mines.edu) as your login.
2. Confirm your MultiPass password is correct — test at myaccount.mines.edu.
3. Clear browser cookies and cache, then retry.
4. Try a different browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended).
5. Disable browser extensions that may block SSO redirects.

### Account locked after failed attempts
- MultiPass accounts lock after 5 failed login attempts.
- Self-unlock at myaccount.mines.edu → "Unlock Account."
- If unlock fails, contact IT Service Desk: 303-278-4357.

### SSO redirect loop or "Access Denied" error
- Clear all cookies for mines.edu and banner.mines.edu.
- Log out of all Google/Microsoft sessions in that browser.
- If error persists, your Banner role may not be provisioned — submit an access request through the IT portal.

### New student: account not yet active
- Banner accounts activate 48–72 hours after enrollment confirmation.
- Check activation status at myaccount.mines.edu.
- If more than 72 hours have passed, contact the Registrar's Office.

### Two-factor authentication (Duo) prompt not appearing
- Ensure your Duo device is enrolled at duo.mines.edu.
- Check your mobile device has Duo Mobile installed and push notifications enabled.
- See the MFA/Duo knowledge base article for full troubleshooting.

## When to Escalate
- Account locked and self-service unlock fails.
- SSO errors persist after clearing cache and trying alternate browser.
- Role access is missing after more than 3 business days of enrollment.
- Banner shows incorrect student or financial data.
