# Access Provisioning — New Accounts & Role Access

## Overview
All Mines system access is provisioned through the IT Service Desk based on your role (student, faculty, staff, researcher). Some systems require explicit approval from a supervisor or data steward.

## New Account Setup

### New student account activation
- MultiPass accounts are created automatically after enrollment confirmation.
- Activation takes 24–48 hours after the Registrar confirms enrollment.
- Activation email is sent to your personal email on file with Admissions.
- If you have not received an activation email within 48 hours, contact the IT Service Desk.

### New employee account setup
1. HR submits a provisioning request when your hire paperwork is complete.
2. Your MultiPass account is created 1–2 business days before your start date.
3. On your start date, activate at myaccount.mines.edu using the temporary password from your activation email.
4. Complete Duo MFA enrollment immediately after activation.
5. Standard access includes: email, Canvas, VPN, OneDrive. Banner, Workday, and Edify require separate role provisioning.

## Role-Based Access Requests

### Requesting access to a specific system
1. Log in to the IT Help Center at helpcenter.mines.edu.
2. Submit a "Service Request" → "Access Request."
3. Include: system name, required access level, business justification, supervisor name.
4. Your supervisor will receive an approval email — access is provisioned after their approval.
5. Processing time: 1–3 business days after supervisor approval.

### Requesting elevated or admin access
- Elevated access (admin rights, data domain access, reporting access) requires:
  - Supervisor approval
  - Data steward approval (for sensitive data systems like Edify and Banner)
  - IT Security review for admin-level permissions
- Processing time: 3–5 business days.

### Access for temporary or contract staff
- Submit a "Temporary Access Request" — specify the exact access end date.
- Temporary accounts are auto-disabled on the specified end date.
- Extensions must be requested before the expiry date.

## Removing or Transferring Access

### Transferring to a new role/department
- Submit an "Access Transfer Request" with your new supervisor's approval.
- Old role access is revoked; new role access is granted.
- Some systems require your old department to formally release the access.

### Reporting unauthorized access
- If you believe someone has unauthorized access to your account or data, contact IT Security immediately: security@mines.edu or 303-278-4357 (select Security option).

## When to Escalate
- Account not created 48 hours after enrollment or hire date.
- Supervisor approval sent but access not provisioned after 3 business days.
- Urgent access needed for time-sensitive work — call IT Service Desk directly.
